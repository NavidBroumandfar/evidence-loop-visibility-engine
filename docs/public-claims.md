# Public claims

Safe claims made by this repository:

- it validates and routes synthetic evidence deterministically;
- it preserves lineage and records an atomic, auditable proposal receipt;
- it can stop cleanly or block without changing a site;
- its deterministic public conformance suite reports committed-case pass rate
  and zero external calls/cost.

Claims this repository deliberately does not make:

- ranking, indexing, traffic, conversion, answer inclusion, or citation
  causality;
- a universal or proprietary GEO score;
- special AI markup, an AI text file, or `llms.txt` as a visibility guarantee;
- autonomous growth, publishing, backlink creation, or provider automation;
- production readiness for credentials, websites, or private data.

SEO, AEO, GEO, and LLMO are useful lenses for bounded observation. They do not
turn a proposal into proof of an external outcome.
